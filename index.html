What is Spotter?

Spotter is a computer vision-based, hands-free tracking and social platform designed for strength athletes. No more wearables or manual input – Spotter seamlessly records your workout data in real-time, transforming how you track, measure, and share your progress.

  • Automatic Tracking: Hands-free rep, set, and weight tracking using AI.
  • Strength Analysis: Track and improve your performance over time.
  • Social Platform: Connect, compare, and compete with friends.
  • Goal Setting: Set and reach personalized strength goals with data-driven insights.

The Future of Fitness Tracking

With a booming fitness tech market and rising demand for data-driven strength training, Spotter meets the need for precision tracking in the weight room. Using cutting-edge AI, Spotter captures complex movements without wearables, offering insights traditional fitness trackers can’t match.

How It Works

  1. Setup Cameras – Spotter partners with gyms to install AI-powered cameras that capture your movements accurately.
  2. Workout Normally – No wearables needed. Just lift, and Spotter tracks every rep, set, and weight automatically.
  3. See Your Data – Access in-depth performance insights and workout summaries through our app.
